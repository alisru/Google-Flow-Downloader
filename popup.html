Google Flow Bulk Downloader

Applies to the two buttons below. Not offered for the whole library, since that can mean fetching hundreds of large video files into memory before it can write a single zip.
Walks every project you have and downloads all media as separate files. Can take a while for large libraries and may need one round of tuning if Flow's internal data shape has changed (details show up in the extension's service worker console).
Open a Google Flow project tab to get started.
